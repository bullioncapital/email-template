Deflation might also be coming to the UK - and it could have a huge impact on business. Inflation rates are heading below 1pc in the UK the lowest level in modern times.
All of this could affect the psychology of how markets work and adjustments would need to be made. Markets will have to stop expecting companies to increase sales year-on-year. Expectations for rising interest rates will be on hold for a very long time. Firms and individuals will become nervous of debt. Governments will be fearful of debt as debt to GDP ratios rise. Small companies will win over majors.
